*Inception* (2010) is a science-fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. It follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets from deep within the subconscious during the dream state. When offered a chance to have his criminal record erased, Cobb and his team must perform the impossible — **inception**: planting an idea into someone’s mind.
